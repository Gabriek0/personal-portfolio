Building a personal portfolio has never been easier. With frameworks like Next.js and the power of MDX for content, you can create a site that's both performant and a joy to maintain.

## Why Next.js?

Next.js offers server-side rendering out of the box, which means your content is indexed by search engines from the first load. The App Router brings file-based routing and layouts that make organizing your project intuitive. Add in built-in image optimization and you have a solid foundation for any portfolio.

## Content with MDX

MDX lets you write Markdown with JSX components. This means you can embed interactive elements, custom callouts, or code demos directly in your blog posts. The combination of simplicity and power makes it ideal for technical writing.

## Key Takeaways

- Use the App Router for clean, nested layouts
- Leverage MDX for rich, component-enhanced content
- Optimize images with next/image for better performance
- Keep your content in version control alongside your code

Whether you're a developer showcasing projects or a creative sharing your work, a modern stack like this will serve you well.
